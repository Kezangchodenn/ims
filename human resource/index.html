Human Resources

Processing personnel data and assisting with the hiring process are the two key responsibilities of human resources administration. This group fosters a productive workplace that is in line with the purpose, guiding principles, and culture of the institution. The top HR-related duties include:

  • Applying and interpreting HR policies and procedures.
  • Keeping departmental procedures and best practices up to par with Bhutanese standards.
  • Encourage the creation of employee individual work plans and discussion of such plans (IWPs).
  • Management and assistance of performance reviews.
  • Create management reports for human resources to support successful college activities.
  • The planning of significant events and gatherings.
  • Immigration, work permits, and business trip administration help.
  • Setting up the necessary arrangements for VIP visits.


The group is in charge of acquiring services, tools, and products that support the business' everyday activities in the administrative function.
